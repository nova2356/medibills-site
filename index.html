Privacy Policy

```
MediBills does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

MediBills does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on-device using local storage. No data is sent to any server or third party by the app. Any backups are created locally on your device and shared only at your explicit direction — we never have access to your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter — including providers, visits, bills, and payments — is stored locally on your iPhone using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back it up yourself using the in-app tools.

Third-Party Services

MediBills does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect user data running in the app.

Biometric Data

The app uses Face ID / Touch ID for authentication. Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS and Apple's Secure Enclave. MediBills never has access to your biometric information.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are used only to remind you of upcoming or overdue bill due dates. Notification permissions are handled by iOS and can be revoked at any time in your iPhone Settings.

Backup & Export

The backup and export features allow you to save your data to your own device storage, iCloud, or any destination you choose via the iOS share sheet. This is entirely under your control. We do not receive, access, or store copies of your data at any time.

Children's Privacy

MediBills does not collect data from any users, regardless of age. The app has no account system, no network requests, and no data collection mechanisms of any kind.

Disclaimer

MediBills is a personal finance tracking tool for organizing medical bill information. It is not a medical service, medical device, or healthcare provider. Nothing in this app constitutes medical or financial advice.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your awareness of the updated policy.
```
